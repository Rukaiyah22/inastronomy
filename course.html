Our Facilities

We take the pride of offering state-of-the-art facilities designed to foster a dynamic learning and research environment. Our institute boasts cutting-edge observatories equipped with advanced telescopes and observation tools, allowing students and researchers unparalleled access to the celestial wonders. In addition, our well-equipped laboratories provide hands-on experience in astrophysical experiments and data analysis.
Inastronomy's library is a treasure trove of astronomical literature, housing an extensive collection of books, journals, and research publications. The institute also offers modern classrooms and lecture halls equipped with multimedia resources, facilitating interactive and engaging learning experiences. For our research scholars, InAstronomy provides dedicated research spaces and collaboration areas, encouraging innovative thinking and knowledge exchange. Our computing facilities feature high-performance clusters, enabling complex simulations and data-intensive research projects. Moreover, InAstronomy places a strong emphasis on fostering a sense of community. We provide comfortable accommodations for students and visiting scholars, creating a supportive living environment conducive to focused studies. Our campus also hosts a planetarium, organizing regular shows and events open to the public, promoting astronomy education in the broader community. At InAstronomy, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of astronomers and astrophysicists by providing access to exceptional facilities that inspire learning, encourage research, and drive innovation
